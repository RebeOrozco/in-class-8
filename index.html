Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Blossom Cookies

Meaningful Eats-Erin Collins

Total Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour blend (with xanthan gum)
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Granulated sugar, for rolling
  • About 36 chocolate kisses or chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Mix together peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy. Then beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together gluten-free flour, baking soda, and salt. THen add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients
  4. Roll dough into balls and roll into granulated sugar. Place on baking sheet apart from each other.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until edges are set. When done baking, immediately press a chocolate kiss.
  6. Cool cookies on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
